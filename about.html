Who I Am

I'm Kojo Greg — a Software Engineer specializing in scalable, data-driven systems that solve complex operational and organizational challenges. My work sits at the intersection of full-stack development, backend architecture, and large-scale data infrastructure.

Over my career I've designed and shipped enterprise platforms that organizations depend on daily — HR and appraisal systems deployed across government agencies, agricultural data platforms processing millions of records, and analytics infrastructure serving thousands of concurrent users. These aren't toy projects — they're mission-critical systems that need to be correct, fast, and reliable.

My engineering philosophy emphasizes performance, security, clean architecture, and reliability. I care deeply about system design decisions because they compound — good architecture makes everything easier; poor architecture makes everything harder, especially at scale.

I'm based in Accra, Ghana and work with clients and organizations worldwide. Driven by curiosity and a genuine love of building things that work, I'm always exploring emerging technologies and looking for problems worth solving.
